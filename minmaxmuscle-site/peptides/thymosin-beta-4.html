TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4)

Research peptide associated with tissue repair, inflammation signaling, and cellular migration.

At a Glance

Category

Regenerative / recovery research

Primary Focus

Wound healing, angiogenesis, and cytoskeletal repair pathways.

Current Status

Investigational; not approved for over-the-counter use.

Overview

Thymosin beta-4 is a naturally occurring peptide involved in cell migration, actin regulation, and tissue repair processes. TB-500 is a synthetic fragment commonly referenced in research settings.

Research Themes

  • Cell migration and tissue remodeling after injury.
  • Inflammation modulation and angiogenesis signaling.
  • Preclinical models evaluating recovery and repair outcomes.

Safety & Regulatory Notes

TB-500 is not approved for clinical use. This information is provided for educational purposes and should not be treated as medical advice or guidance for use.
