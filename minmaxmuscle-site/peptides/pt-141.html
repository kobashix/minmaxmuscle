PT-141 (Bremelanotide)

Melanocortin receptor agonist studied for sexual function and motivation pathways.

At a Glance

Category

Neuropeptide / melanocortin agonist

Primary Focus

Sexual desire and arousal signaling research.

Regulatory Status

Prescription-only in approved formulations.

Overview

PT-141 (bremelanotide) is a synthetic peptide derived from melanotan pathways that targets melanocortin receptors. It is researched for its central nervous system effects related to sexual motivation and arousal.

Research Themes

  • Melanocortin receptor signaling and neurochemical response.
  • Clinical studies on sexual dysfunction and desire pathways.
  • Adverse event monitoring in clinical formulations.

Safety & Regulatory Notes

Bremelanotide is regulated and should only be used under medical supervision. This overview is educational and does not provide medical advice.
