Peptide Stack Playbook

Stacks group multiple peptides to target a theme like recovery, metabolic support, cognitive performance, or skin health. The summaries below are paraphrased, high-level descriptions of what each stack is known for online.

Commonly Referenced Stacks

Stack names are community shorthand. Ingredients and intended outcomes vary across sources, and none of this is medical guidance.

  1. GLOW Stack

    Typically combines GHK-Cu, BPC-157, and TB-500 for skin repair and tissue recovery focus.

    Dermal + Recovery

  2. Wolverine Stack

    Usually centers on BPC-157 with TB-500 to emphasize tendon and soft-tissue recovery.

    Recovery

  3. "God Mode" Nootropic Stack

    Often references Semax, Dihexa, and Selank for cognitive focus and neuro-support themes.

    Neuro

  4. Fountain of Youth Stack

    Commonly pairs Epitalon with GHK-Cu to highlight longevity and skin elasticity narratives.

    Longevity

  5. Growth Hormone Triple Stack

    Often combines CJC-1295, Ipamorelin, and Sermorelin to support GH signaling goals.

    GH Axis

  6. Phoenix Stack

    Combines BPC-157, TB-500, CJC-1295, Ipamorelin, and GHK-Cu for comprehensive recovery claims.

    Recovery

  7. GLP-1 Enhancement Stack

    References Semaglutide or Tirzepatide with CJC-1295, Ipamorelin, and AOD-9604 for weight loss support.

    Metabolic

  8. Vitality Wave Stack

    Uses a Fountain of Youth blend with CJC-1295 to emphasize energy, vitality, and anti-aging themes.

    Longevity

  9. Recovery & Healing Stack

    Combines BPC-157, TB-500, CJC-1295, and Ipamorelin for multi-angle repair support.

    Recovery

  10. Fat Loss & Metabolism Stack

    Often pairs Tirzepatide with Tesofensine and 5-Amino-1MQ to target appetite and lipolysis claims.

    Metabolic

  11. Body Composition Stack

    Semaglutide, AOD-9604, and MOTS-c appear together in stacks aimed at fat reduction and energy.

    Metabolic

  12. Immune Resilience Stack

    Thymosin Alpha-1 and LL-37 are commonly paired for immune defense positioning.

    Immune

  13. Autoimmune & Inflammatory Stack

    Thymosin Beta-4, KPV, and BPC-157 are grouped for immune modulation and inflammation claims.

    Immune

  14. Gut Health & Inflammation Stack

    BPC-157 with KPV is a common pairing for gut barrier and inflammation support.

    Recovery

  15. Muscle Growth Power Stack

    5-Amino-1MQ, CJC-1295, and Ipamorelin are linked to hypertrophy and recovery narratives.

    GH Axis

  16. Cognitive Enhancement Stack

    Semax, Dihexa, and NAD+ are grouped for focus, memory, and brain-energy themes.

    Neuro

  17. Tesamorelin + Ipamorelin Combo

    A two-peptide pairing often cited for visceral fat reduction and GH signaling support.

    GH Axis

  18. Longevity & Anti-Aging Stack

    Epitalon, Thymalin, and GHK-Cu highlight telomere and immune-aging discussions.

    Longevity

  19. Advanced Anti-Aging Stack

    Epitalon, Ipamorelin, Tesamorelin, and GHK-Cu are combined for broader longevity narratives.

    Longevity

  20. Ultimate Peptide Stack

    Retatrutide, BPC-157, TB-500, CJC-1295, Ipamorelin, GHK-Cu, and NAD+ appear in all-in-one blends.

    Multi-Target

  21. Sexual Optimization Blend

    PT-141 with Oxytocin is referenced for arousal and connection-focused outcomes.

    Neuro
