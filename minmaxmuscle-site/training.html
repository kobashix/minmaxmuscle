Weekly Training Split

Built for progression, recovery, and long-term aesthetics. This is not random. This is engineered.

1. Monday – Legs + Core ⋯

Warm-Up

  • Stairmaster – 5 min progressive
  • Hip openers + hamstring floss – 2–3 min
  • 1–2 light leg press ramp sets

Main Work

  • Seated Leg Curl (Machine): 4×10–12, slow negatives
  • Leg Press (Horizontal): 4–5×12–15, controlled
  • Hack Squat (Machine): 4×8–12
  • Leg Extension: 4×12–15, last set partials
  • Hip Abduction: 3×20, constant tension
  • Rear Kick: 3–4×10–12 each side
  • Crunch Machine: 3×12, slow exhale

Conditioning

  • Treadmill: 20–30 min, incline 12–15
2. Tuesday – Upper Torso Maintenance ⋯

Focus

Maintain size, reinforce joints, keep traps and arms active without recovery debt.

  • Shrug (Smith): 5×6–10, 2–3 sec hold
  • Shrug (Smith – Stretch): 4×12–15
  • DB Shrug Hold (Timed): 3×30–40 sec
  • Seated Cable Row (Bar Grip): 4×10–12
  • Lat Pulldown: 4×8–12
  • EZ-Bar Curl: 4×8–10
  • Incline DB Curl: 3×10–12
  • Hammer Curl (Cable): 2–3×12–15
  • Arnold Press: 3×8–10
  • Rear Delt Reverse Fly: 3×15–20

Additional days (Posterior Powerhouse, Hypertrophy & Grip, Pull, Push) follow the same structure. This layout is intentionally modular.
