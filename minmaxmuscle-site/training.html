Train With Intent

This is not a random split. This is a system engineered to maximize progress while minimizing wasted effort.

Training Goals & Design Philosophy

This program is built around a very specific set of goals. Every exercise, rep range, and rest period exists for a reason.

  • Visible hypertrophy in priority muscle groups
    Upper traps, arms, upper chest, and posterior chain are emphasized for long-term aesthetics.
  • Strength progression without joint burnout
    Heavy work is focused where it pays off most, with volume and intensity managed deliberately.
  • Sustainable fat loss while maintaining muscle
    Conditioning supports a calorie deficit without sabotaging training performance.
  • Longevity and repeatability
    This system is designed to be run continuously, not survived once.

Random workouts feel productive. Structured training produces results.

Monday – Legs & Core •••

Focus

Structural leg strength, joint integrity, fat-loss support.

Workout

Warm-up: Stairmaster 5 min progressive + hip openers
Seated Leg Curl – 4×10–12 (slow eccentrics)
Leg Press – 4×12–15 (controlled)
Hack Squat – 4×8–12
Leg Extension – 4×12–15 (partials last set)
Crunch Machine – 3×12
Treadmill Incline Walk – 20–30 min

Lat Pulldown – 4×8–12
EZ-Bar Curl – 4×8–10
Rear Delt Fly – 3×15–20
